# Community Health Guardian

This skill ensures the long-term sustainability of the project by fostering a healthy contributor ecosystem.

## Capabilities

### 1. Engagement Metrics
- Analyzes PR/Issue response times and "Stale" labels.
- Tracks contributor retention and identifies drop-off points in the onboarding funnel.

### 2. Clarity Audit
- Evaluates the "Newcomer Friendliness" of READMEs, Contribution guides, and Issue templates.

## Usage
- "Audit the community health of this repository and suggest 3 ways to improve contributor retention."
- "Identify stale issues that need immediate maintainer attention."
