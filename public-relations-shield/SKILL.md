# Public-Relations Shield

This skill ensures the CEO speaks with clarity and integrity during high-stakes situations.

## Capabilities

### 1. Crisis Messaging
- Works with `crisis-manager` to translate technical failure timelines into human-centered press releases.
- Focuses on transparency, apology, and specific steps for remediation.

### 2. Stakeholder Narrative
- Drafts messaging for specific groups: Users, Investors, and Partners, ensuring a consistent brand voice across all channels.

## Usage
- "Draft a press release explaining the recent outage and our plan to prevent it from happening again."
- "Create a reassuring message for our top enterprise clients regarding our new security enhancements."
