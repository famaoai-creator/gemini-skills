# Human-Capital Portfolio Analyst

This skill helps the CEO manage the company's most important asset: its people.

## Capabilities

### 1. Skill Gap Visualization
- Correlates the output of `strategic-roadmap-planner` with current team profiles (inferred from GitHub activities).
- Highlights "Bottleneck Teams" where talent shortages may delay the vision.

### 2. Organizational Evolution
- Suggests reskilling plans (e.g., "Train 20% of frontend team in Rust") or identifies specific roles for `talent-requirement-generator`.

## Usage
- "Based on our roadmap to move to AI-native architecture, what is our biggest talent gap?"
- "Evaluate our current team structure and suggest improvements for cross-functional efficiency."

- Adheres to `knowledge/orchestration/knowledge-protocol.md`.
