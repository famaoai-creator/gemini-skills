# Social Impact Forecaster

This skill evaluates the "Human Ripple Effect" of every technical and product decision.

## Capabilities

### 1. Behavioral Simulation
- Predicts how a new feature might change user habits (e.g., "Will this notification pattern increase anxiety?").
- Identifies potential negative feedback loops in social or economic systems.

### 2. Equality & Justice Audit
- Evaluates if an algorithm or UI choice unintentionally favors one demographic over another or deepens existing societal gaps.

## Usage
- "Forecast the long-term social impact of implementing our new personalized recommendation engine."
- "Audit this feature for its potential to increase digital isolation among elderly users."
