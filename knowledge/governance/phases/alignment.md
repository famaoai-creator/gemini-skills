# Phase: Alignment
## Goal
Intent interpretation and definition of Victory Conditions.

## Directives
1.  **Intent Declaration**: Interpret the Sovereign's request into actionable goals.
2.  **Context Ranking**: Identify relevant knowledge files (TOP-7 rule) to minimize noise.
3.  **Strategy Formulation**: Create a clear TASK_BOARD or plan before execution.
4.  **Sovereign Switch**: Determine the mode (Governance-First or Autonomous-YOLO) based on the request.

## Constraints
- **Zero Physical Change**: Do not modify project files during the alignment phase.
- **Sudo Gate**: Any decision involving risk (level >= 4) or architectural change requires explicit Sovereign approval.
- **Contract Integrity**: Every mission must have a defined contract or Victory Conditions before proceeding.
