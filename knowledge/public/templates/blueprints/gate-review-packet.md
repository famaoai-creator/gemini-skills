# Blueprint: Gate Review Packet

## 1. Gate Summary
- Gate name
- Target phase
- Decision date
- Decision owner

## 2. Readiness Assessment
- Exit criteria checklist
- Completed deliverables
- Missing deliverables

## 3. Risk & Issue Summary
- Open critical risks
- Open issues
- Dependencies affecting go-live

## 4. Quality & Evidence Summary
- Test status
- Security status
- Operational readiness
- Evidence references

## 5. Decision Proposal
- Go
- Conditional Go
- No-Go

## 6. Conditions & Follow-ups
- Required actions
- Owners
- Due dates

## 7. Approval Record
- Approvers
- Final decision
- Timestamp
