# Gemini Skills: The High-Fidelity Autonomous Engineering Ecosystem

## 1. The Core Problem
Most AI "agents" today are just fancy wrappers around chat prompts.
- **Brittle**: Fail at the first iFrame or complex UI.
- **Indecisive**: Ask "which one?" for every minor trade-off.
- **Flat**: Treat documents as simple text, losing all design and context.
- **High Maintenance**: Require constant human "babysitting."

## 2. Our Solution: The Gemini Philosophy
We don't build assistants. We build **laborers** governed by a **harness**.

### A. Logic as a Hygiene Factor
In our world, logical consistency is the bare minimum. We don't celebrate passing a regex; we treat failure there as an anomaly. Logic is for the machine; it should be silent and perfect.

### B. Vision as the Compass
When logic offers multiple "correct" paths, we don't guess. We consult the **Sovereign's Vision**—the emotional and aesthetic core of the project.

## 3. The Technology Stack (The "Surgical" Tools)

### Bulletproof Browser Navigator
- **Penetration**: iFrame, Shadow DOM, and Multi-tab awareness.
- **Interaction**: Drag & Drop, precise Scroll, and Dialog control.
- **Collaborative**: `ask_human` for MFA/Captcha—true human-in-the-loop.

### Digital Archaeology (doc-to-text Reborn)
- **Soul**: Meaningful Markdown extraction.
- **Mask**: Aesthetic DNA (coordinates, fonts, colors).
- **Context**: Deep metadata and tone analysis.

### Dynamic Feedback Loop
- **Self-Healing**: Automatic log analysis and task board injection.
- **Autonomy**: AI fixes its own environment before you even notice.

## 4. Business Impact (ROI)
- **10x Velocity**: Engineers move from "authors" to "architects."
- **Sustainable Autonomy**: Real-time cost and autonomy tracking (Vital Monitor).
- **In-Repo Governance**: Prompts and rules live with the code (`WORKFLOW.md`).
